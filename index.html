Elissabat

Top 3 Facts About Elissabat!

She’s a Famous Vampire Actress

Elissabat is best known for starring in the hit vampire film “Romance in the Shadows.” In the monster world, she’s basically a glamorous movie star with a devoted fanbase.

She’s Actually Royalty

Elissabat’s real identity is Veronika Von Vamp, a vampire princess. She was hidden away for her own protection and lost her memories for a time, not realizing her true royal status.

She’s Closely Connected to Draculaura

Elissabat and Draculaura are cousins. Their storylines intersect in Monster High: Frights, Camera, Action!, where they uncover Elissabat’s royal past together.
